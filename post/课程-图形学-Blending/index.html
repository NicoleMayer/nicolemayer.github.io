Compositing Functions

Transparency

  • Opaque fragments have $s_a = 1$
    • Framebuffer gets overwritten
  • Transparent fragments have $s_a= 0$
    • Framebuffer remains unchanged
  • Translucent fragments have $0 < s_a < 1$
    • Color gets blended
